Our History

i2ispecialist Technologies Pvt. Ltd."Founded in 2018, i2i Specialist Technologies Private Limited aims to bridge the gap between industry requirements and educational offerings in the IT sector. Since its inception, i2ispecialist has established itself as a leading provider of IT education, website development, web application development, digital marketing, data science, and job placement services within the IT industry."

Growth in IT services (2021-2022): "Building on its success in IT education, i2i Specialist Technologies expanded its service offerings to include professional website development, catering to businesses seeking to establish a strong online presence. Leveraging its expertise in cutting-edge web technologies, the company delivers customized, responsive websites that meet the unique needs of each client

"Simultaneously, i2i Specialist Technologies ventured into web application development, focusing on creating scalable and efficient solutions tailored to streamline business processes for its clients. The expert team of developers at i2i Specialist Technologies employed cutting-edge technologies and agile methodologies to deliver high-quality, custom web applications that enhance productivity and drive business growth."

"Recognizing the importance of digital marketing in today's competitive landscape, i2i Specialist Technologies expanded its offerings to include comprehensive digital marketing services. These services encompass SEO (Search Engine Optimization), PPC (Pay-Per-Click) advertising, social media marketing, and content marketing strategies, all designed to boost client visibility, increase website traffic, and drive sustainable online growth."

Focus on Data Science and advanced job placement services (2023-Present): As data-driven insights became increasingly pivotal for business decision-making, i2ispecialist Technologies ventured into the field of data science. The company began offering training in data analytics, machine learning, and artificial intelligence, equipping students with the skills to analyze large datasets and derive actionable insights.

"Moreover, i2i Specialist Technologies expanded its scope to include specialized job placement services. Recognizing the challenges faced by graduates and professionals in securing suitable employment opportunities, the company forged strong partnerships with top IT companies across various sectors. Through its job placement services, i2i Specialist Technologies facilitates career opportunities for its trained candidates, ensuring a seamless transition into the IT workforce and enhancing career prospects."
