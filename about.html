WELCOME TO i2i SPECIALIST TECHNOLOGIES

i2i Specialist Technologies Private Limited is a preferred Software Development & Digital Marketing Company in Pune.

Our History

i2ispecialist Technologies Pvt. Ltd. was founded in 2018 with a vision to bridge the gap between industry requirements and educational offerings in the IT sector. Since its inception, i2ispecialist has carved a niche for itself in the realm of IT education, website development, web application development, digital marketing, data science, and job placement services within the IT industry.

Expansion into Services (2021-2022): Building on its success in education, i2ispecialist Technologies expanded its service offerings to include website development, catering to businesses looking to establish a strong online presence. The company leveraged its expertise in web technologies to deliver customized, responsive websites that meet the unique needs of each client.

Simultaneously, i2ispecialist ventured into web application development, focusing on creating scalable and efficient solutions tailored to streamline business processes for its clients. The team of developers at i2ispecialist Technologies employed cutting-edge technologies and agile methodologies to deliver high-quality web applications.

Recognizing the importance of digital marketing in today's competitive landscape, i2ispecialist Technologies also started offering digital marketing services. This included SEO (Search Engine Optimization), PPC (Pay-Per-Click) advertising, social media marketing, and content marketing strategies aimed at enhancing client visibility and driving online growth.

Focus on Data Science and Job Placement (2023-Present): As data-driven insights became increasingly pivotal for business decision-making, i2ispecialist Technologies ventured into the field of data science. The company began offering training in data analytics, machine learning, and artificial intelligence, equipping students with the skills to analyze large datasets and derive actionable insights.

Moreover, i2ispecialist Technologies expanded its scope to include job placement services. Understanding the challenges faced by graduates and professionals in securing suitable employment opportunities, the company established strong ties with IT companies across various sectors. Through its job placement services, i2ispecialist Technologies facilitates career opportunities for its trained candidates, ensuring a seamless transition into the IT workforce.
