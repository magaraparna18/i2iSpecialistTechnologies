All You Need to Know About...

i2i Specialist Technologies Private Limited is a trusted Software Development & Digital Marketing company in Pune..

1. What is i2i Specialist?

i2i Specialist is a premier training institute offering specialized courses in Data Science, Data Analytics, Software Testing, Full Stack Development, and Digital Marketing.

2. Who are the instructors at i2i Specialist?

Our instructors are industry experts with extensive experience in their respective fields. They are dedicated to providing high-quality education and hands-on training.

3. Do you provide placement assistance?

Yes, we offer comprehensive placement assistance to our students, including resume building, interview preparation, and direct placement opportunities with our partner companies.

4. Are there any prerequisites for enrolling in a course?

Prerequisites vary by course. Some courses may require basic knowledge of programming or specific tools, while others are designed for beginners. Please check the course details for specific requirements.

5. What kind of certification do you offer?

Upon successful completion of our courses, students receive a certification from i2i Specialist Technologies, which is recognized by industry professionals and companies.

6. What is the mode of training?

We offer both online and offline training sessions to cater to the needs of all our students.

7. How can I enroll in a course?

You can enroll by visiting our website, selecting the desired course, and following the enrollment process. You can also contact us directly for assistance.

8. Do you offer any financial aid or scholarships?

Yes, we offer various financial aid options and scholarships to eligible students. Please contact our admissions office for more details.

9. Can I get a demo class before enrolling?

Yes, we offer demo classes for some of our courses. Please contact us to schedule a demo session.

10. What is the duration of the courses?

The duration of our courses varies depending on the program. Please check the specific course details for more information.

11. What kind of projects will I work on during the course?

Our courses include hands-on projects that reflect real-world scenarios, allowing students to apply what they have learned in practical settings.

12. How can I get in touch with student support?

You can contact our student support team via email, phone, or through our website's contact form. We are here to help you with any queries or issues you may have.

13. Can I switch courses after enrollment?

Course switches are subject to approval and availability. Please contact our admissions office to discuss your options.

14. Do you provide course materials?

Yes, we provide comprehensive course materials, including notes, tutorials, and access to online resources.

15. How often are new courses added?

We regularly update our course offerings to keep up with industry trends and demands. Please check our website for the latest updates on new courses.
