Data Science

Data Science is a multidisciplinary field that combines statistical analysis, machine learning, and data visualization to extract actionable insights and knowledge from complex data sets. The process starts with the collection of data from diverse sources, including databases, online platforms, and sensors. This data is then meticulously cleaned to handle missing values, remove duplicates, and ensure accuracy, setting the stage for advanced analytics and decision-making. Data Science empowers businesses and organizations to make data-driven decisions and drive innovation.
